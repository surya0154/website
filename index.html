MS DHONI

Mahendra Singh Dhoni is an Indian professional cricketer who was captain of the Indian national cricket team in limited-overs formats from 2007 to 2017 and in Test cricket from 2008 to 2014.
He is a right-handed batsman & wicket-keeper.
Suresh Raina and MS Dhoni were not just great teammates, but also wonderful friends.
From playing together for India to representing Chennai Super Kings to announcing their international retirements on the same day, Raina and Dhoni share a great bond.
